# Trophy Wife Supply Editorial Clusters

## Core Principles
- Every article must support a live collection, product family, or merchandising theme.
- Every article must link upward to a revenue page and sideways to at least two adjacent guides.
- Publish around commercial intent first, then broaden into brand and lifestyle coverage.

1. Launch category page for hats and index supporting subcollections.
2. Publish three hat-supporting articles linked to hats, best sellers, and gift shop.
3. Add apparel and accessories cluster pages before publishing long-tail supporting articles.
4. Refresh top-performing articles seasonally and swap featured products based on inventory and merchandising priorities.
